# Sansu UX Tone

## Read First

1. `CONSTITUTION.md`
2. `docs/07_ui_design_guideline.md`
3. `docs/design_review_checklist.md`
4. The relevant diff or target file

## Tone Rules

- Do not blame, command, or rush the child
- Keep copy short and easy to parse
- One message should carry one intent
- Success should feel warm, not noisy
- Errors should explain the next safe action

## Watch For

- Harsh words like `まちがい`, `ダメ`, `失敗`
- Strong imperative phrasing
- Competition framing
- Adult-centric or overly formal wording
- Long or overloaded messages

## Rules

- Preserve meaning while softening tone
- Apply the same tone standard to Japanese and English copy
- Do not make copy cuter at the expense of clarity
